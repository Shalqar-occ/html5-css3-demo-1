<h1 style="color:Tomato;"><b>2. Antivirus</b></h1>
<h3 style="color:orange;"> <b>Definition</b></h3>
<p style="color:blue;"><b>Software that is created specifically to help detect,<br> prevent and remove malware (malicious software)</B>.</p>
<p style="color:black;">Antivirus is a kind of software used to prevent,<br> scan, detect and delete viruses from a computer. <br>Once installed, most antivirus software runs<br> automatically in the background to provide real-<br>time protection against virus attacks.</p>
<p style="color:green;">Comprehensive virus protection programs help <br>protect your files and hardware from malware <br>such as worms, Trojan horses and spyware, and<br> may also offer additional protection such as<br> customizable firewalls and website blocking.</p>
